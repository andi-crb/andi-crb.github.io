Compassion

What are your thoughts about the compassion training?

I think developing compassion is important - but I'm sceptical that this is the best way to do it. I think respectfully listening to others' experiences is more important than meditation or repeating phrases.

Give a short summary of each of the components of the SCARF model of social behaviour

  • Status is your relative importance or seniority. It can be relative to your former self, as well as to others, and that is an easier aspect to increase.
  • Certainty is important for a sense of confidence and stability, especially on a macro level.
  • Autonomy is a level of control over your own life and environment.
  • Relatedness is how we percieve others to be on our side or not - and we often approach strangers with caution.
  • Fairness can have a huge impact on people's actions, often rating more highly than other forms of gain - such as financial reward.
